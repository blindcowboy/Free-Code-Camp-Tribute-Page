Who is Aubrey Gross

Aubrey was born in 1980 in Waco Texas. She was reading by the time she was two and a half years old and soon there after realize that her goal in life was to become an author. By the time she entered school she was convinced she was going to college and did everything she could to achieve that goal. She graduated eighth in her class from Conley High School in Chalk Bluff Texas in 1999.

Aubrey Attended Hollins University in Virginia and The university of north Texas in Denton Texas for her bachelors degree graduating in 2004. She went on to receive a masters degree in writing popular fiction from Seton Hill University in the fall of 2008.

Aubrey now works full time as a marketer and writes romance novels part time. You can find her author page here.
